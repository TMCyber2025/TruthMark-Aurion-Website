About the System

TruthMark-Aurion delivers tamper-proof digital evidence integrity for criminal and civil cases, human rights investigations, and wrongful conviction prevention.

Core features include chain of custody assurance, cryptographic integrity hashing (SHA-256), AI-powered evidence processing, secure packaging and reporting (PDF, DOCX, ZIP), and professional-grade court admissible reports.
